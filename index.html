About Mr Gas

A legacy of trust and reliability in Meyerton

Welcome to Mr Gas, your number one source for all things gas related. For over three decades, we've been the go-to gas supplier for Meyerton's homes, businesses, and tradespeople.

What started as a passion for providing quality gas products and exceptional service has grown into a cornerstone of our local community. Our customers return to us year after year because they know they can count on us for honest advice, competitive pricing, and products that are always in stock when they need them.

We're dedicated to providing you the best service possible, with an emphasis on quality gas, gas accessories, and industrial gas accessories. Whether you're a long-time customer or visiting us for the first time, you'll experience the same friendly, knowledgeable service that has made Mr Gas a Meyerton institution.

Our Mission

To provide clients with quality products and excellent services that exceed customer expectations in the gas industry.

Our Vision

To provide clients with a complete gas solution that completely suits their business needs.

Our Goal

To ensure total customer satisfaction in both technical and service areas while continuously improving our service.
